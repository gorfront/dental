# Andreasyan Dental — Design System

## Brand
- **Name**: Andreasyan Dental
- **Location**: Vagharshapat, Armenia
- **Tagline**: "Where Art Meets Dentistry"

## Colors
- **Deep Teal** (primary): `#0B6E72` — oklch(0.48 0.09 195)
- **Teal Light**: `#14A0A6`
- **Warm Gold** (accent): `#C9A84C` — oklch(0.72 0.12 78)
- **Soft White** (background): `#F8F9FA`
- **Deep Navy** (text): `#0D1B2A`
- **Muted**: `#94A3B8`

## Typography
- **Display**: Playfair Display (headings, hero text)
- **Body**: DM Sans (UI, paragraphs)
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono (data/codes)

## Style
- Premium, clinical-luxe aesthetic
- Glassmorphism cards with subtle teal borders
- Gradient meshes: teal → navy in dark areas
- Particle/blob animations on hero
- Asymmetric layouts — not cookie-cutter grids
- Smooth staggered reveal on scroll
- Micro-interactions on buttons and cards

## Spacing
- Section padding: 5rem vertical
- Card gap: 1.5rem
- Generous whitespace between sections
